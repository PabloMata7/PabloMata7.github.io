End of Life - Play Station Talents 2023 Finalist

End of Life is a Third Person Shooter game developed using C++ and Unreal Engine 5, as a Master's degree thesis at U-Tad university. I worked as a Gameplay programmer and being the lead UI programmer.

  • Worked on:
  • C++
  • Unreal Engine
  • UI
  • Combat & Gameplay

End of Life is a 3rd person action shooter where you become a demi-goddess, imprisoned in a modular rotating city. Slay and fight your way to freedom using your lethal needles, while navigating through the gravity-changing environments of the city.

Due to the gameplay design, where the main character has to fight and move in many diferent rubik faces and gravities, we had to design and implement our own navmesh, AI movement creating a Navigation Grid, and a gravity system, being such a motivating challenge.

As Gameplay programmer, I created and implemented the Shooting and the Bullet Time ability. Additionally, I played a main role on UI implementation, being the UI Lead Programmer during the late development process.
